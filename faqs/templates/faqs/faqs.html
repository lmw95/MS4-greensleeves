Frequently asked questions

On each of our plant pages, there will be a simple guide on how to care for your new plant. We give our customers the rundown on each plant's needs including light, temperature, humidity, air and ease of care.

Your plant will need a pot, water and possibly some plant food (depending on the species). But do not be afraid to treat your plant to some pruning tools, spray bottles or dust clothes if you wish! We provide some suggested items on the bottom of each page.

Here at Greensleeves, we aim to make plant care as simple and easy as possible. When you browse our search pages, we have a number of categories to help you narrow down your selection.

Your plant will arrive fully potted in the appropriate soil - this soil type is stated in your delivery pack. It will arrive in clear, plastic pot to allow for proper drainage, which will need to sit in a tray or an external pot of your choice.

Delivery is £3.20 for orders under £40 and free for orders over £40.

We deliver UK-wide. Due to Brexit and COVID-19 restrictions there may be certain areas we cannot reach, but this will be displayed upon checkout.

For our customers security, we take payment by credit or debit card. We are hoping to allow customers to use PayPal in the near future.
