Environment

Here at Greensleeves, we are dedicated to creating a greener world without costing the Earth (figuratively and literally). We are dedicated to following our No Net Initiative, reaching the following goals:

  • For our supply chain and organisation to have zero-net impact on the climate
  • To reduce our carbon emissions by 40% by 2030
  • Use fully electric delivery vans by 2040
Ethics

With the backing of science, it us a responsibility of all businesses to ensure their practices are in line with current ethical consumerism and environmental impace guidelines. We have ensured we meet these standards by:

  • Ensuring our growers do as little damage to native wildlife as possible
  • Ensuring accessibility in our staffing practices
  • Ensuring our exotic plants are being sourced responsibily from our trading partners
Everyone

It is one of Greensleeves firm beliefs that plant-parenthood is a joy to be shared. We encourage carers from all walks of life and backgrounds to get involved as there should be no barries to enjoying greenery in the home. We:

  • Encourage buyers to carefully choose plants within their experience level - we cater from complete beginner to experienced carers
  • Provide accessibility ramps for disabled buyers in our store
  • Ensure that our website meets accessibility standards
Evergreen

There is nothing more disappointing that unboxing your new plant baby and finding it crawling with mealy bugs, dead leaves and broken stems. At Greensleeves, we want our customers to recieve the highest quality plants possible by:

  • Conducting thorough checks on our plants before they leave our nurseries and greenhouses
  • Ensure your orders are packed securely and safely on our delivery vans
  • Offer full refunds on any damaged plants
